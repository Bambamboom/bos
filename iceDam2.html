Signs of Ice Dams

Primary Signs

Roof ice, ice in your gutters, ice on roof edges, ice on or in your walls are all signs of ice dams causing damage to your home!

Icicle Formations

One of the most simple indicators of ice dams is icicle formations. The larger they are, the more water is trapped behind ice on the roof. With continuous freezing and thawing, these icicles will continue growing in size and length.

Visible Roof / Gutter Ice

Visible ice accumulation on the edge of your roof is a very clear indicator, and likely turning severe. More accumulation here means more water into your roofing, but also your interior walls as well.

Ice in Soffit / Soffit Vents

Ice accumulation in or on your property's soffit means the water has begun to pool inside of your structure, with a high probability of leading to rot in siding, roofing, and interior walls. And eventually, mold.

Ice on Siding, Walls, or Window Frames

Severe damage is very likely at this stage! We strongly advise you schedule an emergency ice dam removal to prevent further damage. Your home or business has water trapped within the structure that will continue to thaw and freeze, developing mold and rotting away and your structure.
